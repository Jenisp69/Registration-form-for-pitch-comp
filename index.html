Guidelines & Rewards

  • Eligibility: Exclusively open to +2 students who have recently passed or are currently studying.
  • Grand Prizes Tap to Reveal
    • 1st Place: NPR 10,000 + 50% Scholarship for 1st Year at NPI.
    • 2nd Place: NPR 7,000 + 30% Scholarship for 1st Year at NPI.
    • 3rd Place: NPR 4,000 + 20% Scholarship for 1st Year at NPI.
  • Assured Perk: Every single participant receives a guaranteed 10% discount if they choose to study at NPI!
  • Presentation Format: Max 10 minutes to showcase your project idea, followed by an interactive 5-minute Q&A session with the judges.
  • Team Size: Form a group of up to 4 members (Solo entries are also welcomed/encouraged).

Prize Pool Distribution Details

Cash awards are allocated per winning group, not per individual, and can be divided among members freely. However, scholarships and waivers are granted per individual. If multiple members from a winning group choose to enroll, every individual member receives the full designated scholarship waiver instead of splitting the percentage.
